Skills

The skills we acquire are not just tools for our toolbox, but also seeds we plant to grow a better future.

I have acquired many valuable skills through my diverse work experiences as a car wash and construction worker, missionary, and metal worker. These include a strong work ethic, problem-solving skills, and the ability to adapt quickly to new environments. In addition, my experience as a missionary taught me the importance of communication and collaboration. At the same time, my time as a metal worker allowed me to develop technical skills and attention to detail. My varied experiences have given me a unique talent set that can be applied to any new challenge or opportunity.

  1. SCC Energy Services, Inc.

      Worked as a logistics team member at SCC Energy Services.

    • Supply Chain Management: I have a strong understanding of supply chain management, including procurement, inventory management, and distribution of goods and materials.
    • Transportation Coordination: I have experience coordinating transportation logistics, including the scheduling of trucks, the management of routes, and the tracking of deliveries.
    • Safety Compliance: I am highly knowledgeable about safety regulations and compliance requirements, and have experience ensuring that safety protocols are followed throughout project sites.
    • Technical Proficiency: I am highly proficient in the use of logistics management software and other technical tools used in the industry.

  2. Fabulous Freddy's Car Wash

      Detailer and supervisor at Fabulous Freddy's with over 75 employees.

    • Attention to Detail: As a detailer, I have honed my attention to detail, ensuring that every vehicle is cleaned to the highest standard. This attention to detail has also been invaluable in my role as a supervisor, where I have been able to spot potential issues and address them proactively.
    • Team Leadership: As a supervisor, I have gained extensive experience in team leadership, including recruiting, training, and managing a large team of employees. I have also been responsible for setting performance targets, conducting performance evaluations, and providing ongoing coaching and feedback to team members.
    • Communication: As a supervisor, I have developed excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, which have been critical in my interactions with team members, customers, and senior management.
    • Sales and Marketing: I have developed a strong understanding of sales and marketing, and have been involved in developing and implementing promotional campaigns to attract new customers to the car wash.

  3. Missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

      Worked as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Seattle, Washington and served in multiple positions of leadership.

    • Teaching and public speaking skills: As a missionary, I had to give sermons, lessons, and presentations on a regular basis.

    • Cultural sensitivity and awareness: As a missionary, I had to respect and appreciate the culture, customs, and beliefs of the people I interacted with. This experience taught me the importance of being culturally sensitive and aware, as well as how to build bridges of understanding and tolerance.
    • Service and volunteerism: I had to serve and volunteer in various capacities, such as helping with community events, providing humanitarian aid, or assisting people in need. This instilled in me the value of service and volunteerism, as well as how to make a positive impact in the world.

  4. Specialized Manufacturing, Inc.

      Worked as a technian for Specialized Manufacturing.

    • Quality control and assurance: In my role, I was responsible for ensuring that the products produced met strict quality standards. I gained experience in inspecting, testing, and measuring components and products, as well as identifying and resolving quality issues.
    • Technical skills: As a technician in a metal manufacturing company, I developed a range of technical skills, such as operating and maintaining machinery, reading and interpreting blueprints and schematics, and troubleshooting technical issues.
